# Market Sizing Template

## 1. Definition
- Product / service
- Geography
- Customer segment

## 2. TAM (Total Addressable Market)
- Units or customers
- Price or ARPU
- TAM = units × price

## 3. SAM (Serviceable Available Market)
- Realistic subset of TAM based on:
  - Geography
  - Regulation
  - Tech constraints

## 4. SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)
- Market share achievable over 3–5 years
- Rationale for share assumption

## 5. Cross-checks
- Top-down vs bottom-up
- Industry reports vs company disclosures
