# Equity-Research
This repository showcases complete and modular components of equity research work, including industry analysis, financial modeling, competitive benchmarking, and valuation writeups. It reflects real-world analytical processes used in evaluating prospective investments.

## Purpose:
This repository highlights:
- Investment thesis development
- Clear articulation of financial and industry insights
- The ability to turn complex models into actionable recommendations
- Strong communication and presentation capability
- These skills directly support private equity workflows, including deal evaluation, diligence, and portfolio review.

## Contents: 
### Full Equity Research Reports: 
- Institutional-grade research created for competitions and academic industry evaluations
- Includes thesis development, risks, industry structure, valuation, and catalysts

### Industry & Market Analysis:
- Market sizing frameworks
- Competitive landscape mapping
- Operating model breakdowns
- Supplier/distributor chain analysis
- Benchmarking of margins, growth drivers, and capital intensity

### Valuation Tools
- DCF models
- Comparable company analysis
- Revenue & EBITDA forecasting
- Scenario and sensitivity tables
- Risk evaluation summaries

### Presentation Materials
- Investor-style slide decks
- Executive summaries
- Pitch outlines

*Written valuations defended before panels of industry professionals (full equity research report for Global Equity Research Challenge, sponsored by CFA Institute)*
