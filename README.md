# Equity Research & Valuation Portfolio

This repository showcases institutional-grade equity research work including 
**valuation models, deal analysis, investment memoranda, and financial modeling tools**.

It is structured to demonstrate the core analytical competencies expected in 
investment banking, private equity, and equity research roles: 
- Financial statement analysis  
- DCF & comparable company valuation  
- Scenario and sensitivity analysis  
- Market sizing & industry research  
- Operating model construction  
- Investment memo preparation  
- Presentation and pitch development  

---

## Repository Structure

### Reports
Full research reports, including the CFA Institute Equity Research Challenge submission and structured analysis artifacts.

### Models
Professional-grade valuation models:  
- DCF  
- Trading & transaction comps  
- Scenario / sensitivity analysis  
- Capital structure analysis  
- LBO-lite model  

### Industry Analysis
Templates for market sizing, competitive analysis, unit economics, and strategic positioning.

### Investment Memos
PE-style quick screens, one-page investment theses, and 3-minute deal summaries.

### Presentations
Pitch decks and presentation templates used to present findings to investment committees and judges.

---

## Purpose

This portfolio demonstrates:
- Ability to evaluate businesses rigorously  
- Build valuation models from scratch  
- Synthesize qualitative + quantitative insights  
- Prepare PE-style investment materials  
- Communicate findings clearly to decision-makers  

It reflects both **academic excellence** and **real-world investment experience**.
