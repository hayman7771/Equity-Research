# Investment Thesis Template

## 1. Executive Summary
- Company name, ticker, sector
- Recommendation (Buy/Hold/Sell or Invest/Pass)
- Key valuation outputs (target price, IRR, MOIC)
- Thesis in 2–3 bullet points

## 2. Business Overview
- Products / services
- Revenue model
- Geographies
- Customer base
- Management & ownership summary

## 3. Industry & Market
- Market size (TAM / SAM / SOM)
- Growth drivers
- Key trends
- Regulatory or technological forces
- Competitive landscape (brief)

## 4. Financial Analysis
- Historical revenue growth
- Margin profile (gross, EBITDA, net)
- Cash flow generation
- Capex intensity
- Working capital dynamics
- Key KPIs

## 5. Valuation
### DCF Summary
- Key assumptions (growth, margins, WACC, terminal growth)
- Implied enterprise value range
- Sensitivity commentary

### Comparables Summary
- Peer group
- EV/EBITDA, EV/Sales, P/E
- Where the company screens vs peers

## 6. Risks & Mitigants
- 3–5 key risks
- Concrete mitigants or monitoring actions

## 7. Investment Conclusion
- Final recommendation
- Time horizon
- Key catalysts
- What would change your view
